Dungeon Crawler User Instructions

 

The Game Dungeon Crawler is a game about exploring floors in a dungeon. By moving a character, you transverse floors, and fight monsters. You play as a group of 4 hero's selected from 6 classes.  These heroes can attack, defend, and cast magic. As you explore the floors, you gain gold to buy equipment and heal to full. Floors are traversed by a figure.

 

Classes:

 

Class |  Attributes

| Warrior |

High strength, and medium speed. High damage class, but is slower than other damage classes. |

| Rogue |

High Speed, and medium strength. fast class, allowing it to attack quicker. |

| Monk |

Balance strength and speed, less damage than warrior, and slower than rogue, but more balanced. |

| Black Mage |

High intellect, and medium speed. High damage class, uses attack magic to damage enemies. |

| White Mage |

High Speed, and medium strength. fast class, allowing it to attack quicker. |

| Gray Mage |

Balance strength and speed, less damage than warrior, and slower than rogue, but more balanced. |

 

Character Creator:

 

You are prompted initially with 6 radar buttons, each representing one of the classes. Select the class you want, and enter in a name. Repeat process four times to create a party of four. After four party members are selected, you will be prompted with a confirmation screen.

Overworld:

 

The overworld is 3 room by 3 room dungeon, consisting of enemy spawners. These spawners produce enemies to be fought in the combat screen, which will be discussed later. Use either WASD or arrow keys to move around.

 

Symbols:

 

Symbol | Meaning  

|  X     |  Wall   |

|  H     |  Player Character   |

|  E     |  Exit   |

 

The exit on each floor leads to the player to the next floor.

Every 9th floor is a Inn and store, which allows the player to purchase equipment and regain health using gold collected from monsters.

 

Combat Screen

 

Every Time an enemy spawner is walked on, a combat screen is generated. on this screen, enemies are located to the left, and heroes to the right. The combat goes by turn, with the fastest hero/enemy attacking first. The player can select attack or defend by using either  the keyboard or the labeled buttons. Enemies are arranged as follows.

 

| 1 | 4 | 7 |

| 2 | 5 | 8 |

| 3 | 6 | 9 |

 

Using the numbers on the keyboard, select your target. Enemies while attack automatically during their turn.

When enough damage has been dealt to the enemy, you win, and then receive gold, XP, and return to the overworld.

 

Menu

 

The menu can be accessed from the over world, and allows the viewing of stats, inventory,and the equipping of heroes.

Each page can accessed by the buttons on the bottom of the page.

Status displays the stats of the four units, including their stats, health, damage, and armor/weapons.

Inventory displays a list of items, that can be sorted. It displays the name, type, and price of each item.

Equip displays the equip screen for each Hero, allowing them to unequip and equip using the buttons. A piece of armor must be selected before using the equip button.

 

Scoring

 

scoring is calculated by examining player levels, and the floor number. Scores are calculated at time of death of the team. Each heroes level is added and scaled up to contribute to the score.
